BACKGROUND

Ntabozuko Enterprise is a newly established company by an experienced individual in the construction field for more than seven years, in 2017 as Pty Ltd Company. It is a 100% Black owned company that seeks to create jobs for the youth in the country as a whole, i.e. the formerly vulnerable groups in our society. It also promises quality services as it is directed by a young, high energized and professionals. We are a Grade 1CE PE status in the CIDB rankings and hold a Level 1 BBBEE status.
